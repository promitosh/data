Part 2:
Get the Raw File Link
Open BG305.xlsx inside your repository
At the top-right → click/place cursor on “Raw”
Copy the raw GitHub link, which will look like:
https://github.com/promitosh/data/raw/refs/heads/main/BG305.xlsx
